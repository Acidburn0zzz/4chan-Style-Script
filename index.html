4chan X

This userscript is intended to be used along side 4chan X. As of now only 2.30+ is supported. Ensure both Auto Hide QR and Persistent QR options are checked in 4chan X's settings.

Known Issues

For exhentai source to work (as intended) in chrome you must install with Tampermonkey or Ninjakit.
